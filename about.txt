This site host a comprehensive database of non-biotope traits of European and North African (Maghreb) butterflies ( 216 traits * 507 taxa)The database is an output from two sECURE workshops kindly supported by sDiv, the Synthesis Centre of the German Centre for Integrative Biodiversity Research (iDiv) Halle‐Jena‐Leipzig (DFG FZT 118; WG 4.28 sECURE).
