    • Who we are and the origins of the project

    The database is an output from two sECURE workshops kindly supported by sDiv, the Synthesis Centre of the German Centre for Integrative Biodiversity Research (iDiv) Halle‐Jena‐Leipzig (DFG FZT 118; WG 4.28 sECURE). The objective of providing a comprehensive database of butterfly traits was to provide a valuable resource for understanding trait evolution, the conservation of butterflies and their responses to past and current environmental changes.

    The database was constructed by: Joseph Middleton Welling1, Leonardo Dapporto2, Enrique García-Barros3, MartinWiemers4,5, Piotr Nowicki6, Elisa Plazio6, Simona Bonelli7, Michele Zaccagno7, Martina Šašić8, Jana Liparova9 & Tim Shreeve1
